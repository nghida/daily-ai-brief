OpenAI Rolls Out GPT-5.2 to Paid Plans

OpenAI is releasing GPT-5.2 — available in Instant, Thinking, and Pro variants — beginning with paid tiers (Plus, Pro, Go, Business, and Enterprise) this week. DeepSeek V4 and Tencent Hunyuan Set for April Launch

China's AI race is accelerating with two significant April releases. These releases signal an accelerating Chinese AI development cycle focused on production-grade applications.

Gemini 3 Pro Tops LM Arena; Claude Opus 4.6 Leads Human Preference

The model benchmark war has a new configuration at the top.
