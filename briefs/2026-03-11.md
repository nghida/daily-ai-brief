**AMI Labs (Yann LeCun) Raises $1.03B for World Models**
AMI Labs, co-founded by Turing Award winner Yann LeCun after leaving Meta, raised $1.03B at a $3.5B pre-money valuation — the largest seed round for a European company. The Paris-based company is building AI architectures based on "world models" that learn from reality and physical environments rather than next-token prediction, targeting healthcare, robotics, and industrial systems. Investors include Nvidia, Temasek, Bezos Expeditions, and Eric Schmidt.
